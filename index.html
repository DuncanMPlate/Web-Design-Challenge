Summary: Latitude vs. X

This project was an analysis of weather patterns relative to the equator. To fulfill this, we pulled live data from OpenWeatherMap with an API call to develop a dataset of over 500 cities around the world

Once we had our dataset, we used Matplotlib to chart 4 different weather factors: Cloudiness, Maximum Temperature, Wind Speed, and Humidity. Here at this site you will find the visual results of our analysis, as well as the corresponding data.
